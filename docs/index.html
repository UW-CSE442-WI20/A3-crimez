How does the distribution of crimes in New York City change over time (2009-2018)?

Overview

Through our interactive visualization, we want to show our users based on historic crime data of 2008-2018, which boroughs in NYC are safest for a person to live in. We show this by asking the question of how the distribution of NYC crime changed over time (2008-2018). This question was at a high level, so we chose to break it down into the following subquestions:

  1. How do the boroughs compare in terms of all crime count for a given year?
    1. In which main premise types did crimes happen?
  2. How do the boroughs compare in terms of the count of a subset of all crimes in a given year?
    1. In which main premise types did crimes happen?
